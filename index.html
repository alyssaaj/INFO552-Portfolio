Alyssa Jordan

My purpose is to be the bridge between development and design.

My UX/UI philosophy is to consider the user and their needs above all else and at every step. Empathy is one of my core principles as without it we end up forgetting the face behind the screen. I believe we must have respect for our users and never break their trust. Users need us to be their advocates, and create design that promotes a better world.
