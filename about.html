About Me

I am a full stack developer who is interested in user experience research and design. I want to see more usability being incorporated in development and create enjoyable user experiences with the applications I code. I graduated from the University of Pittsburgh with degrees in computer engineering and bioengineering. I am currently located in Philadelphia, PA where I am completing a masters in Human-Computer Interaction and User Experience at Drexel University.

I spend most of my time with my dog Zara walking or hanging out in parks. I also love learning new skills. I recently started jiu jitsu and muay thai classes. In addition, I purchased an espresso machine and have been interested in latte art videos.
